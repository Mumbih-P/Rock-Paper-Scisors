• Rock,paper and scissors used to break a tie among friends

    Its played by two players in which each of the played forms one of the three shapes with outstretched hand.

    the shapes are "rock"(a closed fist), "paper"(a flat hand) and "scissors"(a fist with index finger and middle finger extended, forming a V)

    There are three possible outcome: Draw, win or Loss

    If a player plays rock, they beat another player who chooses scissors

    For a win: "Rock crushes scissors", "scissors cuts paper" and "paper covers the stone", otherwise, you lose

    If both players choose the same shape, the game is tied and is usually replayed to break the tie.
